Location

Kakum walklway it is situated in Central Region of Ghana, and is 120 miles of the Accra-Cape Coast road.

Brief History

Established in 1931 as a reserve, it was gazetted as a national park only in 1992 after an initial survey of fauna was conducted.
The national park covers an area of 145 square miles (375 sq km), and named after the Kakum River. This is Ghana's most visited natural attraction.

Trails

The unique canopy trail sits at 130 feet (40 m) in the air taking hikers through the canopies of the tropical forest.
The trail stretches between 7 trees, cross rivers and ravines and covers a distance of 1,150 feet (350 m).
